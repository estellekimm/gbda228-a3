PROJECTS

SPROUT

Sprout is a financial app designed to help new parents manage their family finances with ease while fostering a sense of community and connection.
Developed during the 2024 uXperience Design Jam, hosted by the University of Waterloo Stratford School and Sun Life, Sprout addresses the challenge of guiding young Canadians through the overwhelming financial landscape. Designed in Figma, the app combines practical budgeting tools with gamified elements, allowing parents to set and track financial goals tailored to their family’s needs. By making financial planning engaging and accessible, Sprout empowers parents to save, plan, and grow their wealth while staying connected with others in similar life stages. My design team was proud to have received an Honourable Mention (third place) for our innovative solution.

SOONHARI REBRAND

I developed a comprehensive branding strategy and visual identity for the South Korean alcohol brand, Soonhari. The task at hand was to identify a consumer brand in need of a branding refresh and developed a comprehensive strategy to address its visual and identity shortcomings.
I created a bold, youthful logo using a unique and eye-catching font to better represent the dynamic nature of the brand. I also redesigned the product packaging, creating custom illustrations in Procreate and Adobe Illustrator enhancing the overall unity and originality of the design.
